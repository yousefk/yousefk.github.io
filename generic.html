Levels of Service

We offer 3 base tiers of service, but if your needs do not fit any one tier, we are happy to accommodate your request - just let us know in the order form or by email.

Basic: We will perform an assessment of your website and network remotely, along with recommendations and offers to fix security flaws.

Advanced: We will meet with you in-person and perform more thorough network assessments along with a physical security assessment. Basic fixes are included with the price of the assessment.

Full Checkup: We will meet with you in-person and conduct extensive physical and network surveillance over a specified period of time to ensure that you are secured from the most skilled hackers. Basic to intermediate fixes are included with the price of the assessment.

Other: None of these tiers fit your needs? That's okay. We receive many custom requests and are happy to fulfill them. Just include information in your order form or email on what kind of assessment you would like, and we'll get back to you.

How We'll Contact You

We are very flexible and if you have any preferred method of contact, please just let us know. Otherwise, we will email you and work out a good date/time for a consultation with you. We can discuss your options in person, via phone, or via email, although depending on the services requested, an in-person consultation may be necessary. Our basic service will always be free and does not require an in-person consultation, but do understand that your business may still be vulnerable to hackers.
